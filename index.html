Grass Cutting Semi-Autonomous Rover +

The aim of this project was to design, build and develop a grass cutting mobile robot that can traverse different terrains and environments and incorporate visual AI models to aid it in understanding its surroundings.

The rover prototype successfully passed preliminary testing where it was cutting fixed areas of grass, and navigate its environment and attracting interest from early-stage investors.

3D Design and Printing Electronic Circuitry Visual AI Application

The final aim of the project was to produce a modular robot that can handle multiple outdoor tasks (grass cutting, leaf collection, pesticide spraying,etc) through conveniently remove/equipping the tools required. It was meant to have a robust main frame which the tool kits can then be attached to.

Then equipped with dual frontal cameras and usage of cutting edge visual AI models, the robot can then understand its surrounding and behave accordingly (obstacle avoidance).

Hardware

  • 3D CAD of the chassis and joints which were then printed using Mingda Industrial 3D Printer.
  • Usage of a rocker-bogie suspension system to handle uneven terrain.
  • Aluminium frame to support its weight and 4 dc motors for each wheel along with 4 servo motors for orientation.
  • Adjustable three blades grass cutting disc attached to the bottom of the rover to set desired grass cutting height.
